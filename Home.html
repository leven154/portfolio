My website starts with a landing page based on my actual work mat—filled with little things you’d probably find on my desk. Each object acts as a button that links to a site I think is styled in a cool, unique, or inspiring way. It’s meant to reflect how I work across different mediums, especially how I often start by making something by hand and then turn it into a digital piece that can live online. The rest of the site works as a portfolio, showcasing my real-life projects like sewing, drawing, film, and photography.
